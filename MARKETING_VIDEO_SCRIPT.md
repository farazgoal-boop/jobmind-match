This file is your updated root-level video speech for selling `Shaz Residency Management System` using `JobMind Match` as your client-hunting and outreach dashboard.

It gives you:

1. A simple recording flow.
2. A clean client-facing speech.
3. A full English voiceover script.

5. A closing line for your final shot.

## Part 1: What This Video Should Sell

Your main goal is not to market JobMind Match as a general app.

Your main goal is to show that:

1. You have a real software product called `Shaz Residency Management System`.
2. It is useful for apartments, housing societies, residential towers, gated communities, and property managers.
3. You already have a structured system to find global buyers and pitch them professionally.

## Part 2: Best Recording Flow

Record in this order:

1. Open the dashboard hero section.
2. Show the profile area with your portfolio and platform links.
3. Show the product fields and mention `Shaz Residency Management System`.
4. Show the `Shaz Residency Hunt` button.
5. Click the residency hunt mode.
6. Show the filters switching to property-management targeting.
7. Show the generated links for LinkedIn, Google Clients, and Google Maps.
8. Show the outreach actions like Pitch Email and WhatsApp Pitch.
9. Show the client pipeline section.
10. End on the full dashboard with the residency hunt mode active.
